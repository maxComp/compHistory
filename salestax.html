Sales Tax
Idaho Statesman September 22, 1965

First of 2,000 Idaho sales tax statements are printed on a computer in the office of State Tax Collector Floyd West, left, in preparation for mailing to businesses throughout Idaho.

Watching the machine in operation are Ray Clovis, data processing chief for the office, second from left; Bud Head, of Lybrand, Ross Brothers and Montgomery system consultant, second from right, and Deputy Tax Collector Kenneth Tipton, right. The forms and accompanying letters are assembled later and prepared for mailing by machine at the rate of 7,500 per hour.
