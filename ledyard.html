Richard F. Ledyard



The National Association President Alphonso Pia on the left is presenting the Central Idaho Chapter Charter to Richard Ledyard.

Mr. Ledyard was director of Central Computing at Boise Cascade Corporation from 1957 till 1966. He was born in Chicago and attended Northwestern University. He had previosly worked for Revere Copper in Chicago and Pacific Marine in Seatlle. He moved to Boise in 1957. He was the Central Idaho's Chapter of DPMA first president and later served as it's International Director from 1961 till 1965.
