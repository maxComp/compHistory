State Employees Attend Classes On Univac Use
Idaho Statesman May 22, 1969

Orientation classes in the use of the Univac 494 computer for state personnel tabbed for data processing assignments began this week, Ray A. Clovis, management services director, announced Wednesday.

The classes will involve management as well as technical personnel,, according to William Grover, Univac resident manager.

Classes for State Highway Department personnel began Monday. Other agencies scheduled for orientation between now and June 13 include the departments of law enforcement, health and education and Boise State College.
