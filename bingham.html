Ray D. Bingham



Mr. Bingham, is a native of southern Idaho, he attended Brigham Young University 1961-63. He worked for Westinghouse Electric in Idaho Falls as a Planning Coordinator. He became the Data Processing Manager for Idaho First National Bank in 1964 and was responsible for installation and operations of five computer centers in Boise, Coeur D'alene, Lewiston, Idaho Falls and Twin Falls. In 1969 he became Director of Computer Services for Northwest Orient Airlines.
