Data, Group Will Tour IBM Setup at Ore-Ida
Idaho Statesman December 10, 1963

A tour of the IBM installation at the Ore-lda Foods plant at Ontario is scheduled today for members of the Data Processing Management Association, President Claude Clapsaddle said Monday, Members will meet at 5 p.m, at the Greyhound Bus Depot in Boise, They will eat dinner in the East Side Cafe in Ontario.
