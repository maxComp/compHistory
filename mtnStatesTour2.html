Wholesale Firm Here Plans Tour of Plant
Idaho Statesman February 11, 1964

A tour of the facilities of Mountain States Wholesale Company will be the high point of a meeting of the Central, Idaho Chapter, Data Processing Management Association, Wednesday night, according to Don Marlow a member, of the publicity Committee.

Members will go to the wholesale firm's plant for the tour, conducted by Norton Graham and Gerald Stoor of the company and Howard Crow, a Univac representative.
