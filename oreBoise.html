Ore-Ida Foods Plans to Establish Corporate Headquarters in Boise
Idaho Statesman January 3, 1968

Ore-Ida Foods, Inc., announced plans Tuesday to move its corporate headquarters from Ontario to Boise late this summer. About 75 of the firm's 1,200 Ontario employes will be affected. Ore-Idaho Chairman Frank Armour Jr. said the exact location of the new headquarters in Boise has not been decided but that several locations are under consideration.

The firm's sales and marketing group specialized management talent from other sections of the country and will facilitate company communications and travel, he said. The choice of Boise for Ore-Ida's new management headquarters is largely based on its proximity to company production centers and growing operations in the Ontario and Burley areas, Armour said.
