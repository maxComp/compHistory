Wenden M. Nye


Mr. Nye, CDP, is a native of southern Idaho, he has a B.S in Accounting and Business Law Utah State University 1954. He served two years as commissioned Finance Officer in the U.S. Army Reserve. He worked for Western Equipment Company in Data Processing 1956-1959. Since joining Intermountain Gas in 1959 he has experienced two equipment conversions, first from Burroughs to IBM Unit Record equipment and then from IBM to the Univac 1004.
