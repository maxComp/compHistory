William W. Hunter



Mr. Hunter, CDP was a data processing manager at Boise Cascade Corporation. In 1964 he moved to Portland and opened a data processing department for Boise Cascade. In 1965 he joined the staff of the UNIVAC Divsion of Sperry Rand Corporation. In June of 1969 became Assistant Manager of UNIVAC's State and Local Goverment Marketing Department in In Washington, D.C.
