Improvement Classes Draw Ore-Ida Men
Statesman May 29, 1962

Keeping informed about new machines, new processes and new ideas is taking Ore-Ida officials to special schools and Seminars all over the country. IBM supervisor Wynn Durham completed an IBM course in Seattle on the new 1401 computer on May 11.

Ore-Ida is adding a 1401 to its equipment in June, 1963. May 7-8 saw John Crim and Hugh Rigby, Ore-Ida controller and IBM programmer, attended another 1401 computer school in San Francisco.
