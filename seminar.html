Idaho Statesman November 25, 1968

COMPUTER TECHNOLOGY will be a major topic at a seminar on management information systems Dec. 13-14 at Boise State College. The program for management personnel will be sponsored by the Boise Chapter of the National Association of Accountants in conjunction with the BSC School of Business and Public Administration.

Participating firms are Boise Cascade Corp, and Albertson's, Inc. Representatives of the sponsoring organizations include, from left, Chris G. Daflucas, assistant professor of management; Ross Ruchti of the accountants group; Dr. Robert E. Rose, dean of the School of Business and Public Administration, and Fred Keller, head of computer processing at the school.
