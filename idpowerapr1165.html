Data Group Slates Talk On Computer
Idaho Statesman April 11, 1965

The use of the computing machine in finding answers such as determining service costs and forecasting growth will be discussed when the Data Processing Management Association meets at 7:30 p.m. Tuesday in the Thunderbird Motel.
Maurice E. Byrne, records and study engineer for the Idaho Power Company, will be the speaker. A member of the Idaho Society of Professional Engineers, the National Society of Professional Engineers and other technical groups. Byrne is a colonel in the Signal Corps Reserve.
