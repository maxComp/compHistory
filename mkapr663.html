Morrison-Knudsen Film Showing Dated
Idaho Statesman April 6, 1963

Dee Burlingame and Marvin Robertson of Morrison-Knudsen Company will show a film titled "This is MK" and talk about machine processing used by the company, at a dinner meeting of members of the Data Processing Management Association, beginning at 7:30 p.m. Tuesday at the Thunderbird Motel, according to Tom Minner, the group's secretary.
