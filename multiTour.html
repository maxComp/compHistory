Accountants Plan Study of Computers
Idaho Statesman December 16, 1965

The application of computers in Boise businesses will be studied by members of the Boise Chapter, National Association of Accountants, today at three local concerns.

The trip is sponsored by, the Central Idaho Chapter of the Data Processing Management Association. The accountants group will visit Mountain States Wholesale, 506 South Eleventh; Idaho Power Co., 807 Idaho; and Albertsons Food Centers, 1610 State. The tour will begin at 3 p.m. in the conference room of Mountain States Wholesale.

The visits to the three firms will, be followed by a dinner at the Owyhee Hotel at 6:30. A critique of the tour will be given at the dinner by Wenden Nye, president of the Data Processing group's local chapter.
