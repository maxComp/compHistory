Youths Win Certificates For Course
Idaho Statesman June 6, 1966

Successful completion certificates have been presented to 24 Boise, Borah and Capital high school students who undertook a "future data processors" course this spring. The course was conducted by personnel of the Central Idaho Chapter of the Data Processing Management Association each Saturday from March 19 through May 21, Jim Barnes was the instructor. The certificates were presented by Dr. A. H. Chatburn of Boise College.

Officers of the chapter said the extent of participation justifies another data teaching program next year. Mrs. Barbara Dargaty of Capital High was given special recognition by the association for help in organizing the program. Also Rambo and David Marquart, Capital and Borah instructors, Students completing the course were: Bob Radloff, Leslie Kelley, Timothy Curtis, Gerald Glave, Tim Brown, Raymond Tuttle, John McManus, Christine Hobbs, John McNees, Virginia Williams, Sharon Johnson, Miren Rementeria, Karen Lewis, Gregg Eells, Gary Powers, Kay Dallas, Bill Davis, Katherine Ostrom, Karen Doss John Bartenhagen, Anita Coon, Jill Hallmark, Linda Irvine and Richard Lachmann.
